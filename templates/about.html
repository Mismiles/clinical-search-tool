This site is a clinical serch tool initally made for pharmacists to be able to serach for resources.

The idea is that you are are able to first search for an internet resource

If you cannot find it, you can look at the master list to see if it is under the specified dicipline or category

If you can find the resource, but not using your serach terms, you can edit the search terms by clicking edit.

If you cannot find your resource, you can "add a reource" and fill in the appropriate form.
